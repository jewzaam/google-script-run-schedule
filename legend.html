Event Title

Title is preserved. Emoji added are after a pipe (|) delimiter. Conditiosn can be strung together. For exmaple, the following means there is a chance of thunderstorms and it is humid: ❓⚡💦

Emoji Meaning
❓ Chance of...
☁ Overcast
🌤 Partly Cloudy
🌞 Clear
☔ Light Rain
🌨 Heavy Rain
⚡ Thunderstorm
❄ Snow, Hail, or Ice
🌫 Fog, Haze, or Mist
🌛 Night
🌄 Dawn or Dusk
🌬 Windy
💦 Humid

Event Description

Event description is preserved. Forecast link and conditions are added after three dashes (---).

🌡 Temperature (F)
